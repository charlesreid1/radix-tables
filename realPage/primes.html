Prime Numbers

The following table lists 2000+ prime numbers.

The primes are listed in column-major order, meaning the columns group by the hundreds, and the tens and ones are indexed by row.

For examlpe: to look up the 709th prime, find the 700 column, then scroll down to row 9. The 709th prime is 5381.

(Also, 709 is the 127th prime. 127 happens to be the 31st prime. 31 happens to be the 11th prime, 11 being the 5th prime, 5 being the 3rd prime, and 3 being the 2nd prime. 2 is the 1st prime.)
